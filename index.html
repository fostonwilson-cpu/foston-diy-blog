Project Kickoff Essentials

Lock in the basics before you plug in a tool.

Safety Snapshot

  • Confirm PPE fits and batteries are charged.
  • Review the hazard checklist for electricity, dust, and noise.
  • Stage a charged fire extinguisher within reach.

Material Readiness

  • Lay out cut sheets with scrap allowances.
  • Label bins by step so nothing goes missing mid-build.
  • Dry-fit critical joints before glue or fasteners.

Tool Tune-Up

  • Check calibration on saw fences and torque settings.
  • Swap in sharp blades or bits to save rework time.
  • Set up dust collection and test airflow.
